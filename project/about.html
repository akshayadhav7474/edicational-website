Information and details about the
B.Voc (Software Development program)




    Maharashtra college of infotech and research is started in Jun 2010 & registered under the Maharashtra Tantrik Shikshan Mandal Trust in Ahmednagar. Its a collage in Ahmednagar city, Bachelor of Vocational Studies in Software Development is a course which usually focuses on a skill development programme which concentrates on both theoretical and practical knowledge It includes extensive training and practical experience in software development and computer science This course is made for those students who wish to improve and skills and expertise and earn a decent salaried job. In this course internship sessions are mandatory in various colleges which collaborates with many recognized industries such as Ashok Leyland etc. The students are given a real time practical session to improve their skills in the software department The students are trained with good skills, practical & theoretical knowledge, expertise and experience which helps them for a better future.
