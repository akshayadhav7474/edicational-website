Computer lab

This lab is the pride of our department. This laboratory consists of advance 15 Dell and Lenovo based workstations with 20GB Ram and 1 TB Hard disk that are well equipped to meet the requirements of regular laboratory sessions that are an integral part of the postgraduate curriculum. All workstations have access to both Linux and Windows platforms. All the required software’s are installed with antivirus with periodic updates and maintenance of laboratory are at high priority.

chemistry lab

The Chemistry Department of MTSM's College, established in 1968, is one of the most versatile, dynamic and largest department in the college. It caters to the largest number of Junior as well as Degree College students of Science Faculty and is well-known for its teaching and research accomplishments. The Department is served by a highly qualified, experienced and dedicated teaching faculty along with efficient co-operative non-teaching staff. Four Faculty members – Dr. Ahamad. M.H. Shaikh, Dr. Julekha Shaikh, Dr. Nilima Dixit and Dr. Mayuri Joshi are recognized Ph. D guides in Chemistry at the University of Mumbai.

Physic lab

The Department of Physics is one of the core Departments of the Science Faculty since the College was established in 1968 and is situated at the fifth floor of main building of this institution. It has a very well-equipped laboratories for Degree College as well as junior college students. The Degree College Physics Lab. has an equally equipped dark room attached to it. It also has computers along with the printer and the internet facility. The laboratories are airy, well-lit and have ample space for storing the instruments used in Physics Practical. The stabilized power supply is provided in the laboratories. Sufficient number of electrical boards and switches are available in the laboratories for performing electrical, electronics and optics experiments.
