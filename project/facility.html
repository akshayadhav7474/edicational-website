Facilities



MTSM's COLLEGE OF INFOTECH AND RESEARCH at AHMEDNAGAR is located in Dagadi-Kaman-Behind-Hotel-Parichay-Savedi,Ahmednagar. The state of the art campus provides for all amenities and facilities for students to make them feel welcome at the campus. The institute has well furnished and ventilated classrooms with multimedia tools to aid the learning process.

The classrooms are also equipped with high-speed internet connectivity for interactive learning of students. Apart from this, Bharati Vidyapeeth Deemed University College of Engineering at Pune facilities also include health services through a hospital, library attached, Computer centre, Auditorium, sports equipment and gym for students to participate in extracurricular activities as well as sports.
